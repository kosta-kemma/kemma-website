---

## Content Formate

### LinkedIn Post
```markdown
[Hook - 1 Zeile die stoppt]

[Story/Insight - 3-5 Zeilen]

[Takeaway - was der Leser mitnimmt]

[CTA - soft, nicht salesy]

[3-5 relevante Hashtags]

---
Länge: 100-150 Worte
Tone: Professional aber approachable
```

### Blog Post Structure
```markdown
# [SEO Title - 60 chars]

[Intro - Problem/Hook - 2-3 Sätze]

## [Subheading 1]
[Content]

## [Subheading 2]
[Content]

## [Subheading 3]
[Content]

## Key Takeaways
- [Takeaway 1]
- [Takeaway 2]
- [Takeaway 3]

[CTA]

---
Länge: 800-1200 Worte
```

---

## Tone Guidelines

```
DO:
- Professional aber warm
- Confident aber nicht arrogant
- Educational aber nicht belehrend
- Engaging aber nicht clickbaity

DON'T:
- Zu formal/steif
- Übertriebene Claims
- Zu viele Emojis (max 2)
- Aggressive CTAs
```
